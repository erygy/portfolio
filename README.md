## 1. Desktop AI assistant

A desktop AI assistant that helps me write, dictate, rewrite and translate much faster in daily business exchanges, especially emails, supplier messages and long prompts.

### Problem

I was spending too much time writing, translating and reformulating messages across different tools.

This was especially painful for:

1. Long prompts.
2. Supplier messages.
3. Emails.
4. French to English or Chinese translation.
5. Turning spoken ideas into clean written output.

### Why I built it

I wanted a faster workflow where I could speak once and instantly get the right written output in the right format.

The goal was not to build a public product. The goal was to remove friction from my own daily work and make written communication much faster.

### What it does

1. Dictation from a desktop shortcut.
2. Rewriting spoken French into clean written output.
3. Translation into English and Chinese.
4. Custom output modes for emails, supplier messages and prompts.
5. Clipboard-based workflow to use the output anywhere.

### Status

Used daily.

## 2. YouTube expert chatbot

A chatbot that lets me query expert knowledge from YouTube on specific topics such as SEO, email marketing, business strategy and technical execution.

It uses a database of more than 10,000 indexed videos from more than 100 selected YouTube channels.

### Problem

For fast-moving fields like SEO, email marketing, acquisition or business strategy, the best answer is often not a single universal answer.

Different experts have different methods, frameworks and beliefs. In many cases, what matters is not just getting a generic AI answer, but understanding how a specific trusted expert would think about the problem.

These are also the kinds of topics where people still pay for coaching, consulting or courses, even in the AI era, because they want judgment, method and experience.

### Why I built it

I built it to create an internal expert layer that I could query when I face a precise business, marketing or technical problem.

Instead of manually searching through YouTube videos, courses, newsletters or notes, I wanted to ask a question and get an answer grounded in the content of selected experts I trust.

The goal was to reduce the time between having a problem, finding the right expert perspective and taking action.

### What it does

1. Collects content from selected YouTube channels.
2. Transcribes videos through an external transcription service.
3. Sends the content into a vector store.
4. Lets me ask precise questions based on selected experts.
5. Helps compare different expert methods on business, SEO, marketing and technical topics.

### Status

Used internally.

## 4. Visual ear jewelry configurator

A visual ear jewelry configurator built for Solstice Bijoux.

### Problem

Customers do not buy ear jewelry like they buy a single standard product.

They often need to visualize several pieces together before buying:

1. Which jewelry goes where.
2. Which pieces look good together.
3. Which color or size works best.
4. Whether the final composition feels balanced.

A normal product page does not solve this well.

### Why I built it

I built the configurator to make the buying experience closer to how customers actually think about ear jewelry.

The goal was to:

1. Improve customer experience.
2. Increase buying confidence.
3. Increase average order value.
4. Improve conversion.
5. Capture leads when users save their composition.
6. Make the product discovery experience more interactive.

### What it does

1. Lets users build their own ear jewelry composition.
2. Supports multiple placements on the ear.
3. Handles jewelry variants, sizes and compatibility.
4. Lets users save a composition and leave their email.
5. Helps customers visualize several products together before buying.

### Status

New Shopify version in preparation.

## 5. Shopify inventory and bundle workflows

Backend workflows to manage complex Shopify inventory, bundles and custom product compositions that Shopify does not handle well natively.

### Problem

A custom ear composition can contain several individual jewelry pieces, but the customer needs to see one clean composition in the cart.

At the same time, the business still needs to manage inventory correctly at the component level.

Shopify does not natively handle this kind of custom composition and bundle stock logic well enough for this use case.

### Why I built it

I built backend workflows to make the configurator usable in a real e-commerce context.

The goal was to connect the front-end composition experience with the operational reality behind it:

1. Cart logic.
2. Product composition.
3. Component-level inventory.
4. Bundle logic.
5. Fulfillment clarity.

### What it does

1. Manages custom compositions.
2. Handles bundle logic.
3. Helps keep inventory usable.
4. Creates cleaner cart workflows for customers.
5. Makes the operational side easier to manage.

### Status

Built for internal use.
